Wildcoat: A Story of Quality, Style, and Outdoor Adventure

In the ever-evolving world of outdoor gear, where quality and functionality must meet the rugged demands of nature, there is one brand that stands out for its exceptional craftsmanship, attention to detail, and commitment to providing the best in outdoor apparel. That brand is Wildcoat. Known for its unique designs and versatile range, Wildcoat has quickly become synonymous with durability, comfort, and, most importantly, style in the outdoor community. Their product line, including the iconic Wildcoat Bear Coat and Wildcoat Grizzly Bear collection, speaks to adventurers and nature enthusiasts who seek both functionality and fashion. Explore the collection that embodies our story.

The Wildcoat Journey

2015

Founded in a Garage

Wildcoat was born when founder Alex Johnson couldn't find a winter coat that combined durability with style for his backcountry adventures in the Canadian Rockies.

2017

First Retail Partnership

Our products hit the shelves of a small outdoor gear shop in Banff, marking our first retail presence.

2019

Bear Coat Launch

The iconic Wildcoat Bear Coat was introduced, quickly becoming our bestselling product and a favorite among serious outdoor enthusiasts.

2021

Carbon Neutral Certification

We achieved carbon neutral status across our manufacturing process, reinforcing our commitment to sustainability.

2023

National Retail Expansion

Wildcoat products became available in outdoor retailers across all Canadian provinces.

Our Manufacturing Process

At Wildcoat, we take pride in our Canadian manufacturing heritage. Our coats are crafted in facilities that uphold the highest standards of quality and ethical production. Here's what sets our process apart:

  • Local Materials: We source materials from Canadian suppliers whenever possible
  • Skilled Craftsmanship: Each coat is assembled by experienced artisans
  • Quality Control: Rigorous testing ensures every product meets our standards
  • Sustainable Practices: We minimize waste and use eco-friendly processes
