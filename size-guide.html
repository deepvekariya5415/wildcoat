Winter Coat Size Chart - Find Your Perfect Fit

The Importance of the Wildcoat Winter Coat Size Chart

As the chilly months approach, choosing the right winter coat becomes essential not only for warmth but for comfort and style as well. The Wildcoat winter coat size chart is a helpful tool for finding a winter coat that fits perfectly, ensuring you stay cozy while also looking great. With winter coats often being a significant investment, it's vital to make sure that the coat you choose fits your body shape and size, which is where a reliable size chart plays an important role. View our full collection. For those looking for plus-size options, Wildcoat also offers a variety of plus-size winter coats that combine both function and style.

How to Measure for the Perfect Fit

Follow these steps to ensure accurate measurements for your winter coat:

  1. Chest: Measure around the fullest part of your chest, keeping the tape measure horizontal
  2. Waist: Measure around your natural waistline (where you normally wear pants)
  3. Hips: Measure around the fullest part of your hips
  4. Sleeve Length: From the center back of your neck, over your shoulder, and down to your wrist
  5. Length: For long coats, measure from the base of your neck down to where you want the coat to end

Pro Tip:

When measuring, wear the undergarments you plan to wear with your coat for the most accurate fit.

Fit Tips for Winter Coats

When selecting your winter coat size, consider these important factors:

Layering

If you plan to wear thick sweaters underneath, consider sizing up for comfortable layering.

Arm Mobility

Raise your arms to ensure the coat allows full range of motion for outdoor activities.

Length

Longer coats provide more warmth but may restrict movement for active use.

Hood Fit

Ensure the hood accommodates your head comfortably with or without a hat.
