Whether you're shopping for winter coats, jackets, or accessories, it's essential to familiarize yourself with Wildcoat's shipping, exchange, and return policies to avoid any unpleasant surprises. Shipping Policy Details

Processing Time

All orders are processed within 1-2 business days (excluding weekends and holidays). During peak seasons (November-January), processing may take an additional 1-2 business days.

Tracking Information

You will receive a shipping confirmation email with tracking information once your order has shipped. Tracking updates may take 24 hours to appear in the carrier's system.

International Shipping

We ship worldwide. Shipping costs and delivery times vary by destination. Customers are responsible for any customs duties, taxes, or fees imposed by their country.

Undeliverable Packages

Packages returned to us as undeliverable will be refunded minus the original shipping costs. Please ensure your shipping address is correct and complete.

Return Policy Details

Time Frame

You may return most items within 30 days of delivery. Items must be in new, unused condition with all original tags and packaging.

Return Shipping

We provide prepaid return labels for Canadian returns. For international returns, customers are responsible for return shipping costs unless the return is due to our error.

Refund Process

Refunds are issued to your original payment method within 3-5 business days after we receive your return. Credit card refunds may take 5-10 business days to appear on your statement.

Non-Returnable Items

Final sale items, personalized products, and items marked as non-returnable cannot be returned. Swimwear and underwear must have hygiene seals intact.

Exchange Policy Details

Eligibility

Items can be exchanged within 30 days for a different size or color, subject to availability. Items must be in new condition with all original tags and packaging.

How to Exchange

Contact our customer service team to initiate an exchange. We'll provide instructions and a prepaid return label if applicable.

Exchange Shipping

We cover the cost of shipping the replacement item to you within Canada. International customers are responsible for any additional shipping costs.

Price Adjustments

If an item goes on sale within 14 days of your purchase, we'll refund the difference upon request. Contact us with your order number and details of the price change.
